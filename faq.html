Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying tickets, account management, and event policies.


How do I receive my tickets?

Once your purchase is confirmed, tickets are sent directly to the email address associated with your account. You can also view and download your mobile tickets by logging in and visiting the "My Tickets" section.


Are the tickets on TicketBlaster verified?

Yes! We guarantee that every ticket sold on our platform is 100% verified and authentic. We work directly with event organizers and verified resellers to ensure you get into your event safely.


Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

All sales are final. However, if an event is cancelled by the organizer, you will receive an automatic refund to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.


Why are there service fees?

Service fees help us cover the costs of operating our secure platform, providing 24/7 customer support, and verifying ticket authenticity. We strive to keep these fees as low and transparent as possible.


I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Go to the Sign In page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we will send you a link to reset your password immediately.
