About Us

TicketBlaster began as a simple idea shared by three passionate programmers during a college project. That idea grew far beyond the classroom and evolved into one of the fastest growing ticket platforms today.

We are three women in tech who bonded over our love of live events and our frustration with how difficult it could be to get reliable tickets. What started as a semester assignment quickly transformed into a working prototype, then a full platform, and now a thriving business trusted by fans across the country.

What We Do :

  • Provide verified tickets for concerts, sports, theater, festivals, and more
  • Ensure safe, secure transactions on a platform built from the ground up
  • Deliver clear pricing with no hidden surprises

Why TicketBlaster?

  • Founded by women
  • Built with transparency and user experience at its core
  • Trusted ticket marketplace with verified sellers
